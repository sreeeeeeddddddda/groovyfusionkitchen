# groovyfusionkitchen
Ever wondered what a C-major chord tastes like? I built an interactive web app that's part synthesizer, part lava lamp, and part random recipe generator. You play notes on a digital xylophone, and the app uses the sound and visuals to generate your sick beats and turns them into a cookbook of legendary glowing recipes.
A single click randomizes the entire drum sequencer and dials all the FX knobs to random values.
A toggle that lets users bend the pitch of the synth just by moving their mouse across the screen (like a digital theremin).
play the Arcade Mini-Game: Look at the "Arcade Challenge" box in the sidebar. Tap the exact sequence of numbers shown (for example: 3 ➔ 1 ➔ 8). If you hit them in order, the progress bar will fill up, trigger a full-screen background particle explosion, and spin up a new random challenge.
